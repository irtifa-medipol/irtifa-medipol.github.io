About Us

Our team carries out various studies on autonomous vehicles. Our major area is rotary wing UAVs. As a team, we want to develop useful technologies for our country. There are currently 8 people in our team. We have friends working in the fields of avionics, image processing, deep learning, UAV control, ROS, Embedded software, Native ESC hardware and design in the team. We participate in TEKNOFEST competitions with the UAVs we have developed as a team. We competed as a finalist in the rotary wing category of the 2021 TEKNOFEST International UAV competition. We are preparing for the 2022 Teknofest International UAV competition rotary wing category.

Genesis

The altitude team is a team that was established by the gathering of 3 of our friends who want to work in the field of robotics. We would like to thank them for these steps that seem small but have brought us to this day. After our team was established, we grew up with our friends who wanted to join our team and now we have become a huge community that includes 2 more teams besides our team. All teams in the community are preparing for Teknofest competitions.
